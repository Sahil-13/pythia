8. Risk Assessment & Mitigation
8.1 Major Risks
1. Algorithm Changes (Risk: Medium-High)

Impact: Could reduce reach overnight

Mitigation: Focus on evergreen content, build email list, diversify platforms, prioritize quality over gaming algorithm
​

2. Content Saturation (Risk: Medium)

Impact: Harder to stand out in crowded niches

Mitigation: Target underserved sub-niches, unique storytelling angles, superior production value
​

3. Copyright/Fact-Checking Issues (Risk: Medium-High)

Impact: Strikes could lead to demonetization

Mitigation: Use licensed/royalty-free media, thorough source citation, academic approach to controversial topics
​

4. Burnout (Risk: Medium)

Impact: Inconsistent uploads harm algorithm performance

Mitigation: Batch content creation (record 4-6 scripts in one day), build content buffer, set sustainable schedule
​

5. Slow Initial Growth (Risk: High)

Impact: Discouragement leading to channel abandonment

Mitigation: Set realistic expectations (500 subs = success in Month 1), celebrate small wins, focus on improvement metrics over vanity metrics
​

8.2 Compliance & Best Practices
YouTube Community Guidelines:

Avoid misinformation (thoroughly fact-check history/philosophy claims)

Respect cultural sensitivity (especially with religious/mythological content)

Properly attribute all sources and images

Disclose AI-generated content where required
​

Copyright Strategy:

Use Creative Commons media

License stock footage/music

Create original animations/graphics

Fair use doctrine for educational commentary (with proper citation)
